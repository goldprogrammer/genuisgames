In this simulation, you must destroy the asteroid before it hits the earth.
This may happen in the future, so we need to prepare asteroidnauts like you NOW.
In the simulator, there are two bars at the top.
The first one is red and represents the asteroid's proximity to earth.
The greater the density of the metal, the faster it travels to earth.
The blue one represents how close you are to destroying the asteroid.
There are also two cannons.
The first one is orange with an number 2 on it.
It is extremely powerful, but can only be used after being charged.
You charge it by tapping the blue cannon labled 1.
Good luck, future asteroidnaut.
